We aim to provide a comprehensive STEM curriculum to students aged 8-14. Each session includes an alternation between Science topics and Coding topics. In each session, we'll have fun educational activities to ensure an understanding of the topic. Our sample curriculum for topics is listed below.

Science

  • Session 1: Physics
    • focusing on gravity (what is it? how does it change throughout the universe? how can we model it?)
    • space-time model for gravity demonstration
  • Session 2: Biology
    • DNA and genetics (structure of DNA)
    • making model DNA (out of pipe cleaners)
    • How do our genes contribute to our appearance and personality?
  • Session 3: Psychology
    • younger group: memory (how can we improve our memory? how do we store information in our brains?)
    • older group: sleep and sleep cycles (what are dreams and what is their purpose? what are the stages of sleep? why is each stage important?)
  • Session 4: Chemistry
    • acids & bases
    • experimenting with litmus paper
  • Session 5: Astronomy
    • planets
    • how stars are formed
    • What are black holes?
    • big bang theory
  • Session 6: Earth Science
    • igneous, metamorphic, and sedimentary rocks
    • identifying the different types of rocks

    Coding (Python)

    • Session 1: The basics
      • introduce coding and its significance
      • understanding the basics of Python through fun programs
    • Session 2: int & str
      • write a fun, easy program
    • Session 3: Boolean & comparing
      • fun activities to help kids learn comparing in Python
    • Session 4: If and else statements
      • experimenting with new topics
    • Session 5: Writing a fun program!
      • use the knowledge covered in prior sessions to work on a fun program
