Home

We aim to provide a comprehensive STEM curriculum to students aged 8-14. Each session includes an alternation between Science topics and Coding topics. In each session, we'll have fun educational activities to ensure an understanding of the topic. All supplies will be provided by Project STEMreach, including laptops (for coding purposes). Our teachers are dedicated high schoolers who share a passion for STEM, as well as educating the disadvantaged youth. Our locations + signups are listed below.
